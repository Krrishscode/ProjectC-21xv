Arrow keys -> Movement of your car

shift -> exit from a level

enter (when on home) -> lobby (where you can chose 
levels)

enter (when in level1 and distance === 1000 or more) -> next level i.e. level2

r -> for replay a level

space -> for -> horn 

b -> for after gameover back to lobby
